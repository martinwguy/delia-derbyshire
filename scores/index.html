Delia Derbyshire's scores

22 August 2011

I've tried typesetting a few of Delia's scores from her papers using the 600-pound gorilla of music typesetting, Lilypond, which produces not only beautiful PDF sheet music but also a MIDI file that you can listen to to get an idea of the note sequences.

Here are a few of the more successful ones.

Le Pont Mirabeau

The text is a poem by Apollinaire. Typeset in June 2010.
Lilypond, PDF, MIDI.

Music To Undress To

Made by uniting a mostly-complete score and a separate sheet of bass counterpoint together. Typeset in August 2011.
Lilypond, PDF, MIDI. For a complete list, see the wikidelia's Score category.
